Everette Taylor

Everette Taylor is a multifaceted entrepreneur, marketer, and philanthropist known for his impactful work in the tech industry. Born in Richmond, Virginia, Taylor's journey to success is marked by resilience and determination. Despite facing numerous challenges in his upbringing, including homelessness and financial instability, Taylor's drive and passion for entrepreneurship propelled him forward. Throughout his career, Taylor has held various prominent roles in the tech world. He co-founded the marketing agency MilliSense and served as the Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) for Skurt, a transportation startup that was later acquired by Fair.com. Taylor's expertise in marketing and branding has been instrumental in the success of numerous companies. Beyond his professional achievements, Everette Taylor is also committed to giving back to his community. He actively engages in philanthropic endeavors, particularly focusing on initiatives supporting underprivileged youth and minorities in tech. His dedication to social impact and diversity has earned him widespread recognition and admiration. Taylor continues to inspire aspiring entrepreneurs through his leadership, mentorship, and advocacy for diversity and inclusion in the tech industry.
